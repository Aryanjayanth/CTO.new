## üí° Pro Tips

1. **Use Tags**: Organize notes with consistent tags
2. **Color Code**: Use colors to quickly identify items
3. **Regular Backups**: Export data weekly or monthly
4. **Set Priorities**: Mark truly important tasks as high priority
5. **Review Daily**: Check your timetable each morning
6. **Track Habits**: Start with 2-3 habits, not 20
7. **Use Folders**: Keep notes organized by project/area
8. **Set Due Dates**: Add deadlines to important tasks
